Why Choose Renewable Energy?

Discover the key advantages of switching to clean and sustainable power

Eco-Friendly

Renewable energy sources like solar and wind produce little to no greenhouse gases, helping reduce global warming and air pollution.

Cost-Efficient

While the setup cost can be high, clean energy systems lower electricity bills over time and reduce dependence on imported fuels.

Sustainable

Renewables come from natural sources that never run out, making them reliable for meeting growing energy demands without harming the planet.

SDG 7: Affordable and Clean Energy

Ensuring access to affordable, reliable, sustainable, and modern energy is the heart of Sustainable Development Goal 7.

73%

Energy is by far the main contributor to climate change. It accounts for 73% of human-caused greenhouse gases.

22%

With 98% electricity access nationwide, the Philippines continues to expand its renewable energy sources, which now make up around 22% of its power generation.

2.8B

Almost a third of world's population 2.8 billion rely on polluting and unhealthy fuels for cooking.

Our Objective

Goal 7 is about ensuring access to clean and affordable energy which is key to the development of agriculture, business, communications, education, healthcare and transportation.


Ensuring universal access to affordable electricity by 2030 means investing in clean energy sources such as solar, wind and thermal. Expanding infrastructure and upgrading technology to provide clean energy in all developing countries is a crucial goal that can both encourage growth and help the environment.
